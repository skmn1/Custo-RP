## French typography rules applied throughout

- **Non-breaking space** (`\u00A0`) before `:`, `;`, `!`, `?`
- **Guillemets** `« »` instead of `" "` for quotes
- **Comma** as decimal separator (`40,0`)
- **Narrow no-break space** as thousands separator (`1 234,56`)
- **Space** before `%` (`85,5 %`)
- Capitalise only the first word of titles (`Planification du personnel`)
- Keep accented capitals: `É`, `È`, `À`, `Ç`
